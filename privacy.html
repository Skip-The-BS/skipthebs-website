01 Who we are

Skip the BS is an independent software operation building mobile apps that do one thing well. Our apps are available on iOS and Android. This privacy policy covers all apps published under the Skip the BS umbrella, including NO BS IF.

Questions? hello@skipthebs.app

02 What we collect

Nothing. Our apps do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers because we don't have any servers involved in normal app operation.

All data you enter into our apps — fasting times, streaks, settings — is stored locally on your device using your phone's built-in storage. It never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it or enable optional third-party integrations like Apple Health.

03 Analytics and tracking

None. We do not use any analytics SDKs, crash reporting services, advertising identifiers, or third-party tracking libraries.

We have no idea how many people use our apps, which features they use, or how long their sessions are. In fact, we have no idea if anyone uses them at all. Our friends and family might be lying to us. We chose this deliberately. The alternative requires collecting data about you, and that's not something we're willing to do.

04 Apple Health and third-party integrations

If you choose to enable Apple Health sync (an optional paid feature on iOS), NO BS IF may write fasting data to the Apple Health app on your device. This data is governed by Apple's privacy policy and your own Health settings — we don't receive or store any of it on our end.

We do not integrate with any other third-party services. No Facebook, no Google, no advertising networks.

05 Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not handle, store, or have access to any payment information. All purchase data is governed by Apple's or Google's respective privacy policies.

06 When you contact us

If you email us at hello@skipthebs.app, we receive your email address and whatever you write. A real person reads it and responds. We don't add you to mailing lists, share your address with anyone, or retain your message longer than needed to resolve your issue.

07 Children

Our apps are not directed at children under 13. Since we collect no personal data from anyone, we collect none from children either. If you believe a child has contacted us and shared personal information, email us and we'll delete it promptly.

08 Changes to this policy

If we ever change this policy in a meaningful way, we'll update the date at the top and note what changed. Given that we collect nothing, there isn't much to change. If we ever introduce any form of data collection — even anonymised analytics — we'll be explicit about it and give you a clear choice.
